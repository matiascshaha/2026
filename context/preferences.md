# preferences.md

## Tone
- No fluff
- No cheesy corporate language
- Strong, clear, concise wording
- Sound technically credible

## Positioning
- Emphasize builder mindset
- Emphasize software engineering depth, not just QA execution
- Emphasize architecture, maintainability, internal tooling, automation infrastructure
- Show ownership, but do not exaggerate leadership beyond truth
- Do not make me sound like a manager

## Resume Rules
- Prefer impact-oriented bullets
- Prefer specific technical work over vague responsibility statements
- Do not overuse buzzwords
- Keep bullets tight
- Prioritize the most relevant brag entries for the role
- Do not include weak or irrelevant bullets just to fill space

## Cover Letter Rules
- Keep it grounded and specific
- No generic passion language
- No fake enthusiasm
- Tie claims to real evidence from my background

## Avoid
- “Results-driven”
- “Passionate professional”
- “Dynamic leader”
- generic AI-sounding phrasing
